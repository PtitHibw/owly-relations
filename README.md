# Owly Relations

Owly Relations is an interactive Obsidian plugin that lets you **visualize and manage relationships between people** in a virtual “house.” Move people around rooms, create groups, and track everything with a full history.  

---

## 🚀 Features

- **Interactive House View**  
  - Drag & drop people between rooms.  
  - Zoom (scroll wheel) and pan (hold middle click) on the house layout.
  - Badges show names, colors, and group emojis.  

- **People Management**  
  - Add, edit, or remove contacts.  
  - Assign nickname, first name, last name, color, and groups.  
  - Link a contact to a note and open it with a double click.  

- **Groups**  
  - Create, edit, or delete groups.  
  - Assign an emoji for easy identification.  
  - Add people to multiple groups.  

- **Rooms / Pieces**  
  - Define rooms and their description.  
  - Control visibility of rooms (hidden rooms move people to “outside”).  
- **History Panel**  
  - Track every movement between rooms.  
  - Add comments to any movement.  
  - Filter by person or group.  

---

## 🖱 How to Use

### Moving People
- Drag a person’s badge to another room in the house.  
- The movements are recorded in the history automatically.  

### Selecting People
- Click on the badges to select them.  
- Select multiple persons through the history section's search bar by searching a group or multiple names.

### Editing a Person
- Double-click a badge to open the person's linked note.  
- Modify name, color, groups, or linked note.  
- The badges displays the nickname in priority.

### Managing Groups
- Use the settings panel to create, edit, or delete groups.  
- Choose an emoji for each group for visual cues using an emoji picker.

### Managing Rooms
- Customize the rooms names, descriptions and visibility to your liking.

---

## 📦 Installation

Install and activate OwlyRelations using obsidian's community plugins or :

1. Download or clone the plugin into your vault: .obsidian/plugins/owly-relations/
2. Enable the plugin in **Settings → Community Plugins → RelationshipHouse**
3. Restart Obsidian if necessary.  

---

## ⚠️ Notes

- The `house.svg` file must exist in the plugin’s `assets` folder.  
- Notes linked to people must exist in the vault.
